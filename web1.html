餐馆介绍Our Story

环球食尚 坐落于城市中心，融合现代设计与经典烹饪。自2012年开业以来，我们致力于用当季食材创造令人难忘的味觉体验。优雅的就餐环境与开放式厨房，让每一餐都成为享受。

Global Taste is located in the heart of the city, blending modern design with classic culinary arts. Since 2012, we have been dedicated to crafting unforgettable flavors with seasonal ingredients. The elegant dining room and open kitchen make every meal a celebration.
