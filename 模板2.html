Our Heritage

A Century of
British Excellence

Founded in 1887 by Sir Edmund Harrington, our restaurant has been a cornerstone of London's culinary scene for over a century. We celebrate the rich tapestry of British cuisine — reimagined with contemporary precision.

Each dish tells a story: of English countryside harvests, Scottish highland game, Welsh coastal seafood, and Irish dairy traditions — curated by our executive chef, whose philosophy honours provenance above all.
